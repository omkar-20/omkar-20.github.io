Intranet Privacy Policy

We respect employees privacy

Any personal information an employee provides to Josh Software Private Limited, including and similar to name, address, telephone number and e-mail address shall not be released, sold, or rented to any entities or individuals outside of our organisation. We only provide the information to trusted entities that work on behalf of or with Josh Software under strict confidentiality agreements.

The following personal information is automatically collected by Josh Software when employees login to Josh Software’s Intranet
Email ID

The Intranet can only be accessed by Josh Software employees to authenticate and map users. We ask employees to login through their Josh Software email ID (G Suite for Business).

Calendar and Drive

We use Access tokens and Refresh tokens to call the Google Calendar and Google Drive APIs for scheduling interviews and adding events.

External Sites

We are not responsible for the content of external internet sites. Employees are advised to read the privacy policies of external sites before disclosing any personal information.

Cookies

A "cookie" is a small data text file that is placed in your browser and allows us to recognize an employee each time he/she visits this site (personalization, etc.). Cookies themselves do not contain any personal information, and we do not use cookies to collect personal information. Cookies may also be used by third-party content providers such as Netsfeeds.
